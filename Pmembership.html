Individual Membership

Who Are Our Individual Members?

ARiMaS’ individual membership is restricted to risk individuals who hold key executive positions within their firms, organisations or institutions. They lead the risk agenda in organisations.

Risk leaders are typically senior management, Heads of Risk areas, C-level executives, Chief Risk Officers, board members, senior risk consultants as well as representatives of risk management associations and institutes.



Membership Fees

The annual membership fee is USD 250.

This fee is payable upon application. We cannot begin to process your application until this has been received.

Being an ARiMaS member does not just open the door to exciting opportunities in your career and personal development. It also gives you access to a first-class range of services designed to support you in your professional life.

Member benefits and services

ARIMAS keeps you at the leading-edge of the profession with a range of benefits and services which include:

The ARIMAS designation - the choice among employers

When you’re a member you can use the ARiMaS’ designation after your name – a symbol of excellence and recognisable to employers around the world. Being a member helps:

  • • increase your earning potential
  • • improve your career progression opportunities
  • • provide international mobility to your career.
The chance to be an international influencer and to build an international professional network

Being an ARiMa’S member means that members are regularly invited to play a part in developing the profession at all levels.

You could ultimately create or influence policies that impact on individuals, businesses and communities alike. This can help to raise your profile, broaden your professional networks and develop your skills.

Supporting you in your continuing professional development

We provide all kinds of materials and opportunities to help you in your career and support your continuing professional development.

These include a comprehensive range of exclusive e-learning packages, as well as face-to-face networking events.

In collaboration with some of the world’s leading universities and professional institutions, we’ve also identified courses that complement the ARiMaS’ Certification, the African Certified Risk Manager (CARM).

Through our partnerships, you can benefit from both accelerated access routes and discounted fees on many of these courses, while maintaining our reputation for excellence in professional education.

Keeping you informed – online, in print and on the move

We’ll keep you up to date with what's happening in the risk management world in a variety of ways:

Our member-focused social media platforms

As well as providing members with a forum to engage and network with each other online through our members' only LinkedIn group, we also keep you up to date through our Twitter feeds.

Access to our technical activities and advice section

For the latest technical, policy and research information on a wide range of subjects, browse our technical library.

In return for all the member benefits we provide, we charge an annual subscription. This also helps to support our objective of promoting our members on the continental stage. Your annual subscription is payable on 1 January each year and we’ll send you a notification the November before.

If you’re a new member, welcome. You’ll start paying your annual membership subscription from 1 January following your admission. You’ll also pay a one-off admission fee.

The simplest and quickest way to pay your subscription is online, via myARiMaS, although there are other options available.

Please remember to pay your fees on time to continue your membership.

Managing your membership

To ensure everything continues to run smoothly, please remember to keep your contact details up to date

Please keep your account details up to date, so we can send you important information about new benefits, services and your membership.

Updating your email, job role or organisation is easy. Simply log into my ARiMaS and update them there.

If you’ve changed your name by marriage, please let us know in writing by email and attach a photocopy of the relevant documentation.

Renewing your membership for 2019

Your ARiMaS’ journey continues into 2019. Stay on this journey with us and experience how we continue to shape and lead the profession.

Two steps to keep your membership active

Each year, to keep your membership active, you need to do the following:

Pay your annual subscription. You’ll be removed from the ARiMaS register if this is not completed each year.

A request to renew your membership will be sent out to you in November each year. You’ll then be required to renew your membership on or before 1 January the following year.

How to renew your membership

The easiest way to renew your membership is via your my ARiMaS account. Here you can pay your membership fee with a credit or debit card,

Reduced Fees

Need a little help? ARiMaS offers two categories of reduced Fees:

Extended leave subscription

You may qualify for an extended leave subscription if you earn less than 10.000USD per year and you are not in active employment by being in one of the following categories:

  • • are raising a family full-time or are a full-time career;
  • • have re-entered full-time education;
  • • are experiencing long-term unemployment;
  • • are suffering a prolonged illness that’s prevented you from seeking employment;
  • • have left accountancy to pursue a religious vocation.
Subscription assistance

This is available to members if they are in employment and earn less than 10,000USD per year.

Applications are currently open for the current subscription year, applications for the following year can be made from September onwards. If your application is accepted your fees will be adjusted accordingly on your account and a confirmation email will be sent to you.

Any application which is accepted in advance for the following year will reflect the reduced rate from January onwards.

To continue to be eligible for reduced subscriptions, members will have to apply annually.

Visit our fees and charges section to find the current reduced membership fee rate.

Applying for a reduced subscription

If you would like to apply to pay a reduced subscription, please go to my ARiMaS.
