The African Journal of Risk Management (AJRM) is published quarterly online only on behalf of on behalf of the African Risk Management Society. Additional issues may be published for special events (e.g. conferences) and when special themes are addressed.

This is an open access journal which means that all content is freely available without charge to the user or his/her institution. Users are allowed to read, download, copy, distribute, print, search, or link to the full texts of the articles, or use them for any other lawful purpose, without asking prior permission from the publisher or the author.
